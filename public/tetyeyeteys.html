This webpage was created by Rheazel Cang of Rubidium '29. It depicts the full Friday Class Schedule of the section. On Fridays, the class has about 3 double periods (back-to-back), 2 single periods, and 2 study periods.

Author's Note - Friday is a schedule that personally gives me mixed feelings. One one hand, the three back-to-back double periods really tire me out and leave me too drained to focus well in the later classes. On the other hand, Fridays mean I get to go home. As a dormer, I always look forward to Friday because I get to see my family and pets again. Fridays are fun when class is over.
